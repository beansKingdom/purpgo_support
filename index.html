PurpGo uses device permissions only when necessary and does not collect or upload any personal data: Microphone is used only for speech-to-text; audio is processed by Apple and no voice files are stored. Notifications are used for reminders and are triggered locally. All tasks, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Log files record only technical status and are shared only by your initiative; the developer does not automatically access them.
