PurpGo Privacy Policy

欢迎使用 PurpGo。我们深知个人信息对您的重要性，并会尽力保护您的隐私。本政策将说明我们如何处理您的数据。
Welcome to PurpGo. We deeply understand the importance of personal information and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.
一、我们收集的信息
I. Information We Collect
PurpGo 是一款语音待办事项管理应用，我们充分尊重您的隐私。 1. 当您使用语音输入功能时，应用会请求麦克风权限。音频由 Apple 语音识别服务处理，仅用于将语音转为文字并创建待办，我们不会存储或上传任何语音文件。 2. 当您设置提醒时，应用会请求通知权限，以便在指定时间发送提醒。通知仅由系统本地触发，不涉及任何个人信息的收集。 3. 所有待办事项、设置、偏好等数据均存储在您的设备本地，我们不会收集、上传或备份这些数据至任何云端服务器。 4. 当您使用"分享日志"功能时，应用会读取本地日志文件并交由系统分享，我们不会获取这些文件的内容。
PurpGo is a voice task management app that fully respects your privacy. 1. When using voice input, the app requests microphone permission. Audio is processed by Apple's speech recognition service solely for transcribing speech to text and creating tasks. We do not store or upload any audio files. 2. When setting reminders, the app requests notification permission to send timely alerts. Notifications are triggered locally by the system and involve no collection of personal information. 3. All tasks, settings, preferences, and other data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or backup this data to any cloud server. 4. When using the "Share Logs" feature, the app reads local log files and hands them over to the system share sheet; we do not access the content of these files.
二、信息的使用
II. Use of Information
我们仅将上述权限及数据用于实现应用核心功能： • 麦克风：用于语音转文字，创建待办内容。 • 通知：用于提醒您按时完成待办事项。 • 本地存储：用于保存您的所有数据，确保离线可用。 我们不会将您的任何数据用于商业目的、用户画像或广告投放。
We use the permissions and data solely for core functionality: • Microphone: converting speech to text for task creation. • Notifications: reminding you of pending tasks. • Local storage: saving all your data for offline use. We do not use any of your data for commercial purposes, user profiling, or advertising.
三、信息的存储与安全
III. Storage and Security
您的所有数据仅存储在您的设备中，我们无法远程访问。我们建议您妥善保管设备，启用设备加密及锁屏密码以保护数据安全。 应用内含有数据导出/导入功能，导出后的文件由您自行保管，我们不对导出文件的安全性负责。
All your data is stored solely on your device; we cannot access it remotely. We recommend keeping your device secure, enabling device encryption and a lock screen passcode. The app provides data export/import features. Exported files are under your own custody, and we are not responsible for their security.
